BODY SHAMING AWARENESS

WHAT IS BODY SHAMING?

Body shaming is when people say mean things or make negative comments about how someone looks, especially their size, shape, or weight. It can happen in person, online, or through societal expectations. Body shaming can make people feel bad about themselves and impact their mental and emotional well-being.
